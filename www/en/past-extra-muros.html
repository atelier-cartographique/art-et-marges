The Jewish Museum of Belgium gives " Carte Blanche" to Art et marges museum

22/10/19 - 26/01/20

The Jewish Museum of Belgium (www.mjb-jmb.org) invites the Art et marges museum to take possession of its premises on the occasion of the Brussels Drawing Week. In the form of a "carte blanche", Art et marges will present a selection of sensitive drawings by some emblematic artists, selected from some 3500 pieces of raw and related art from its collections.

Exhibition as part of the Brussels Drawing Week.

Address: 21 rue des Minimes, 1000 Brussels
