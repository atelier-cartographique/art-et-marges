ACTUELLE

Save the world ?

30.09.2016
29.01.2017

In cooperation with:

Atelier Goldstein, Collection ABCD/Bruno Decharme, La galerie christian berst art brut, La Fabuloserie, La S Grand Atelier, The Museum of Everything

ANONYMOUS - EMERY BLAGDON – FRANCOIS DE JONGE - GUO FENGYI - HANS-JÖRG GEORGI – JAKLINE - LA S GRAND ATELIER - DIRK MARTENS - EIJIRO MIYAMA - GIOVANNI BATTISTA PODESTÀ - MELINA RICCIO - ANDRÉ ROBILLARD - JEAN-PIERRE ROSTENNE – GEORGE WIDENER

Design aims to heal incurable diseases, enter cities, proclaim messages of peace, arrange plans to destroy misery, and build shelters to house the population in the event of a global disaster.

Whether upset by tragic event or called by a higher force, the authors presented in this exhibition feel compelled by a mission intrinsically connected with their creativity. They all give their utmost to devise imaginary solutions to repair or save the world.

Exhibition mounted under “MULTI ORDINARY”, the inaugural edition of a festival dedicated to marginal performing arts.

À VENIR

Gustav Mesmer

09.02.2017
11.06.2017

Gustav Mesmer (1903-1994), after being admitted as a patient at a psychiatric hospital since the age of 26, started making drawings, plans and life-size models of flying machines that he invented and tested himself. Some of them were able to achieve take off with the aid of pedals, while others were harnessed to the pilot’s shoulders and required jumping off a high place. During this period, he earned the nickname Icarus of Lautertal, in reference to the valley of Lauter, where he conducted his first test flights.

This exhibition is an immersive experience, showcasing a large selection of the artist’s work, courtesy of the Gustav Mesmer Foundation: machines, photographs and original drawings that will allow you to plunge into Mesmer’s universe.
