Vernissage "Lisières"

19 June, 6:00 – 9:00 PM

Private viewing in the presence of Caroline Lamarche, curator of the exhibition

The Art et Marges Museum has the pleasure of entrusting the curatorship of its summer exhibition to Caroline Lamarche (“Nous sommes à la lisière” [We are at the Edge], Gallimard, Goncourt novella prize 2019). The selection was made from the museum’s reserves, among the works of our rich permanent collection, but not only.

SURPRISE!

An opportunity to re-discover brand new or forgotten treasures through the author’s questioning gaze: We are living in an age of edges: How can we find our way between animal and human, tree and river, feather and skin, concrete and nature? How can we face the future when fragile environments are collapsing?

By what patient gestures, what wild narratives, what surprising sharing? The answer awaits with the artists of the Art et Marges Museum, who deploy their picnic of works in the summer clearing.

Sound performance : Women voices, art faces by Margarida Guia

Sunday 3 February 2019 - 2pm

Surrounded by accessories (sampler, music boxes, melodica, etc.), Margarida Guia sings and chants the words she wraps with her musical composition. Intimate scores or text, word and sound image intertwine. For the exhibition entitled Women in Outsider Art? (Art et marges musée), she offers an experience of sound and poetry to showcase female creativity. She draws her inspiration from the works on show at the Art et marges musée, from the preface of the catalogue by Caroline Lamarche and recordings made during the visits to the museum. An original creation where breathing, silence, words and sounds will interblend.

As part of the Week of the sound.

The performance will be followed by a mediation in the exhibit " Women in Art Brut?"

Free entrance. No registrations.

Bozar presents Sous le soleil de Marseille de Guy Brunet

Wed 7 Feb 2018, 20:00 → 21:30

On the occasion of the Guy Brunet and Josselin Pietri exhibition, BOZAR presents Sous le soleil de Marseille (2017), the latest film by Guy Brunet.

Alone and without financing, Brunet has produced 15 feature films, donning the hat of producer, screenwriter, dialogist, set designer, actor, cameraman, director and poster artist. Sous le soleil de Marseille looks back at the film career of Marcel Pagnol, with the actors and actresses sharing their experiences in conversation with Brunet himself.

Opening of the Alone&Accompanied exhibition

22.06.2017 5pm - 8pm
© Jeanne Bidlot

The strength and beauty of the works of these five artists are obvious. But without diverse collaborations, would their works have seen the light of day? Born from private mentoring or an artistic workshop for people with mental disabilities, they are the result of these encounters. The exhibition combines aesthetic pleasure with reflection, intelligence and… vigilance.

With works from : Sylvain Cosijns, Philippe da Fonseca, Véronique Declercq, Richard Moszkowicz, Pascal Tassini

Conception : Gérard Preszow / Assistant : Magalie Thiaude
