COME BACK

2.10 et 3.10

Art et marges museum is COMING BACK ! Performances, workshops, guided tours, children’s activities… All this 100% positive. COME♡BACK is not a vaccine yet, but let’s make it count as cultural remedy! More informations

WHAT IS HIDING IN THE DARK ? - 2nd of october

This activity is only given in French & Dutch. A monotype print workshop by Laura De Wolf where you can release all your creativity before entering the exhibition “Coeur au Ventre”! Based on the shadows of the artworks, you and your family will invent new objects. Only then will you discover the amazing art & marges originals. Booking

DAILY RITUALS - 3rd of october

After a surprising tour of the exhibition “Le coeur au Ventre”, feast on the performances of the Magical Amator’s Club with Pierre-Patrica Kasses (La gazette à Pépé), Sophie Melis, Chiara Monteverde and Giulia Piana. They turn everyday gestures into sacred rituals: brushing your teeth, cleaning your ears, menstruating. After the performance, stop by Juliette Dana, who will tell you what the cards hold in store for you. Booking

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.
