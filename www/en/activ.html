LAUNCH OF JEAN-PIERRE ROSTENNE’S BOOK + INAUGURATION OF POP-UP STORE

Thursday 29/11/18 7:00 – 9:00 PM

Jean-Pierre Rostenne, wellknown figure in the Marolles quarter and among regulars of the Art et marges musée, finally has his book. Get your copy before anyone else . A great gift idea as the holidays draw near…

... all the more reason why we are combining the launch of this book with the inauguration of our Christmas pop-up store. Unique pieces by artists at friendly prices.

GUIDED TOUR OF THE EXHIBITION “WOMEN IN OUTSIDER ART?” by the collector Hannah Rieger.

Sunday 02/12/18 2pm

“Women in outsider art?” is above all a selection of works from the prestigious collection of Hannah Rieger. During this visit, she will go over the history of her collection (works, artists, favourites) and shed a light on the theme of the exhibition.

2pm visit conducted in English

3:30pm visit conducted in German

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.
