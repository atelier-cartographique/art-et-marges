AGENDA

Opening of the Alone&Accompanied exhibition

22.06.2017 5pm - 8pm
© Jeanne Bidlot

The strength and beauty of the works of these five artists are obvious. But without diverse collaborations, would their works have seen the light of day? Born from private mentoring or an artistic workshop for people with mental disabilities, they are the result of these encounters. The exhibition combines aesthetic pleasure with reflection, intelligence and… vigilance.

With works from : Sylvain Cosijns, Philippe da Fonseca, Véronique Declercq, Richard Moszkowicz, Pascal Tassini

Conception : Gérard Preszow / Assistant : Magalie Thiaude

« Kifékoi ? » Roundtable

22.06.2017 3pm - 5pm

To what extent can artistic, psychological and educational mentoring be a catalyst for such substantially individual forms of expression? These are the questions of the « Kifékoi ? » Roundtable

With the participation of : Chris Delville, De Bolster, Le Créahm-Bruxelles, Le Créahm Région Wallonne, La S Grand Atelier.

Inscription : 02 533 94 90 ou info@artetmarges.be

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.
