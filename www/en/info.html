Philosophy of the museum

Located in the heart of Brussels (Les Marolles), the art et marges museum has been defending, for the last 25 years, the work of artists who are outside the mainstream art world. These self-taught artists, labelled “outsiders”, work either in isolation, or in workshops for mentally disabled and psychologically fragile people. Thanks to the museum’s temporary exhibitions, this type of art -which is still too often ghettoized- has opened up even more to artistic diversity. Outsider and contemporary artists are side by side in this melting pot that is the art et marges museum.
