Georges CAUCHY

(France, 1963 -)

Some people are difficult; we might even call them stubborn! Well, Georges is one such person: very difficult to communicate with, he has nonetheless some truly remarkable bouts of creativity. At such times, every form submits to his will and takes on more intensity. He uses felt-tip pens, which gives luminosity and transparency to his blocks of color. But you are warned: these works are fragile because the felt-tips employed fade in daylight.
